if you've a ready mind,

Houses

  Upon arrival to Hogwarts, first-year students are sorted into one of four Houses, Gryffindor, Hufflepuff, Ravenclaw, and Slytherin. The House system plays an integral role in the Hogwarts experience. Students will eat, live, and take classes with their house. Throughout the school year, students will be rewarded points for sucesses, and given deductions for misbehavior. At the end of the school-year, the house with the most points is rewarded the House Cup!
