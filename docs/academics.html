Academics

  The 1000-time winner of the Daily Prophet's best Wizarding academy worldwide, Hogwarts School offers a wide variety of courses. First year students must take the following core classes: Transfiguration, Potions, Charms, Defense Against the Dark Arts, Herbology, and Astronomy. In their third year, students may elect to add any combination of Muggle Studies. Divination, Ancient, Runes, Mermish, Artithmancy, and Care of Magical Creatures, but must continue taking the 6 core classes through their 5th year, at which point they will take the Ordinary Wizarding Levels (O.W.L.s). In their 6th year, student will continue any courses in which a satisfactory O.W.L. score is achieved.
